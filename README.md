# 🌙 LUNA - Your AI Laptop Matchmaker

Welcome to **LUNA**, a beautifully designed, conversational AI assistant that helps you find the perfect laptop. Instead of scrolling through endless spec sheets, just tell LUNA your vibe, budget, and needs, and she will search the live internet to find your perfect match.

## ✨ Features

* **Live Web Grounding:** Powered by Google's **Gemini 2.5 Flash** and integrated with the official Google Search tool. LUNA doesn't rely on outdated datasets; she pulls real-time prices (in ₹), specs, and current market trends directly from the web.
* **Premium Glassmorphism UI:** A heavily customized Streamlit interface featuring frosted glass containers, smooth CSS-driven chat bubble animations, and an interactive, glowing LUNA avatar.
* **Dynamic State Animations:** The UI reacts to the AI's state. When LUNA is "thinking" and searching the web, her avatar pulses with a custom CSS neon glow.
* **Context-Aware Memory:** Features a persistent, interactive sidebar that tracks your recent queries and remembers the context of your conversation.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend/Framework:** [Streamlit](https://streamlit.io/) (with heavy custom CSS injection)
* **AI Model:** [Google Gemini 2.5 Flash](https://aistudio.google.com/)
* **Capabilities:** Streaming responses, conversational history, and live Google Search grounding.
